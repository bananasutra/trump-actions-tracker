# 🙊 U.S. Democracy Gone Bananas: Trump Actions Tracker

**A real-time strategic diagnostic of systemic democratic erosion and institutional dismantling in the United States since January 2025.**

### 🔍 Project Overview
The **Trump Action Tracker** is a friendly data dashboard designed to monitor the velocity and impact of executive actions on the foundations of American democracy. This tool categorizes policy shifts into ten critical themes, including **Violating Democratic Norms**, **Hollowing the State**, **Suppressing Dissent**, and **Weaponizing Federal Institutions**.

### 📊 Key Strategic Insights
* **Institutional Rewrite:** Tracking the transition from policy disruption to a full administrative overhaul of the federal government.
* **Strategic Velocity:** Analyzing the "procedural shock" caused by high-volume executive orders designed to outpace judicial processing latency.
* **Norm-Collapse Loops:** Identifying "multi-tagged" actions that simultaneously weaken multiple institutional checks and balances.

### 🛠️ Methodology & Data
This dashboard utilizes verified data from the **Christina Pagel / Trump Action Tracker** Source: trumpactiontracker.info (CC BY 4.0). It provides a high-fidelity visualization of the **Rule of Law**, **Civil Rights**, and **Federal Oversight** hollowing through 2026.
